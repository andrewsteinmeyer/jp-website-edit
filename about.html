Meet Alice LoGuidice

MSN, APRN, CPNP-PC, FMCHC

Alice is a nurse practitioner with advanced training from the Institute for Functional Medicine (IFM), the Medical Academy of Pediatric Special Needs (MAPS), and the Functional Medicine Coaching Academy (FMCA).

She built Journey Pediatrics because she kept seeing the same thing: bright, complicated kids falling through the cracks of conventional medicine. Parents told "they'll grow out of it." Labs that never got run. Fifteen-minute appointments for problems that needed sixty.

Alice doesn't do band-aids. She runs the panels, reads the data, and stays with families until the picture makes sense.

With 24 years of clinical experience and a deep personal investment in getting this right — she's also a mother of three — Alice didn't create another medical practice. She created the one she wished existed.

Our Approach

The Functional Medicine Approach

Traditional pediatrics asks: What symptoms do we see? Functional medicine asks: Why are those symptoms there in the first place?

We use the IFM framework — symptoms are the branches, and we work our way down to the roots: genetics, gut health, inflammation, environmental triggers, nutritional status. This isn't alternative medicine. It's thorough medicine.

🔍

Root-Cause Investigation

Advanced lab panels and comprehensive evaluation to understand what's driving your child's symptoms — not just managing them.

🌱

Whole-Child Treatment

Nutrition, environment, gut health, immune function, sleep — we look at every system because they're all connected.
